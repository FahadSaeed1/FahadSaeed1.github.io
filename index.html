About

As a Climate Scientist I am serving in multiple National and International Platforms. Under many Studies regarding Climate change I have a large experience, more than 17 years of Research and writing for vulnerability, mitigation and adaption policies in Climatology, Glaciology, Hydrology and Global warming impacts worldwide. In addition, wrote many articles/blogs and made media appearances in international media outlets.

Got diverse regional work experience in South Asia, African region, Arabian Peninsula, Europe etc. At present, Working with Climate Analytics that is one of the best international thinks tanks for Climate Change science/policy research. Serving as Adjunct Professor at Center of Excellence for Climate Change Research KAU Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Previously worked with well-renowned Government institutes of Pakistan as well.
